TURBO is a Russian development team focusing on adoption of IT innovations and creation of advanced business applications. Our history began back in 1991 with the legendary "Turbo Accountant" program, which quickly won the market. As the needs of customers evolved, our product line grew and expanded. Today, TURBO provides a new line of business apps that are united by a Generation Z concept.
We introduce metaphors of cards, breadcrumbs and tabs to simplify complexity of business entities and processes. The way young professionals typically use global Internet services is now reflected in modern business solutions.

The interface of TURBO is fully focused on the modern user. It helps new hires to speed up onboarding and facilitate their integration into the corporate environment.

TURBO Solutions are successfully used in Russia and worldwide, including China and Hong Kong, France, Ukraine, Kazakhstan, Moldova, Estonia, Cyprus. In 2019 we entered the Hungarian market.
